Hi There!

This is a li'l image recognition app I made to showcase some of my angular skills. The app uses Clarifai's Technology to predict image contents.

App Features:
  • Login & Registration
  • JWT session & validation
  • A "like" feature
  • Image content recognition
  • A debounce function (lazy loading)
  • AWS S3 integration
  • Flexobx layout

Usage

Register as 'user' to be able to view, like & predict Image, or as 'Admin' to also be able to upload new Images.

Login.

Click on a selected Image and await Clarifai's magic.

Click on an image's to like the image.

Enjoy!
